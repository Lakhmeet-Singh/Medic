ABOUT US

Our organization is dedicated to providing reliable and accessible medical information to the public. Our chatbot uses the data from the NHS to provide users with quick and accurate information on various health topics, as well as helping them manage their symptoms and make appointments with healthcare providers.

DATA SOURCE

We have chosen to use data from the National Health Service (NHS) as our data source. This is because the NHS is a trusted and reliable source of health information, and it provides comprehensive data that covers a wide range of health topics. The data is used to provide users with up-to-date information on symptoms, treatments, and procedures, as well as making appointment bookings with healthcare providers.
