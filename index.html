ABOUT US

Medic is a platform that offers an automated doctor called DOCTORBOT, providing users with quick access to medical support for their queries, treatments, and medication recommendations. Medic aims to offer an all-in-one medical system that can provide users with access to healthcare anytime, anywhere. It also provides users with information about nearby pharmacies. Medic's goal is to make healthcare accessible and affordable to everyone by providing reliable and quick medical support to users.

DATA SOURCE

At Medic, we take great care in ensuring that the information provided to our users is accurate and up-to-date. To achieve this, we rely on the National Health Service (NHS) as our data source. As a trusted and reliable UK healthcare organization, the NHS provides us with comprehensive data covering a wide range of health topics. We use this data to offer users the latest information on symptoms, treatments, medications, and nearby pharmacies. We believe that by using data from a reputable source like the NHS, we can provide our users with the most reliable and trustworthy healthcare information possible.

FEATURES

Symptoms

Through our platform, users can get up-to-date information about a wide range of symptoms, helping them to identify and understand their health issues.

Medication

We provide information on prescribed medication and potential side effects, helping users make informed decisions about their treatment.

Pharmacy

Our platform offers information about nearby pharmacies, enabling users to easily locate and access the medication they need for their treatment.

BENEFITS
